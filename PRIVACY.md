# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: February 15, 2026**

At OnHyper.io, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your personal information.

---

## 1. What Data We Collect

### 1.1 Account Information

When you create an account, we collect:
- **Email address** — Used for authentication and communications
- **Password** — Stored as a secure cryptographic hash (we never store plain text passwords)

### 1.2 API Keys

You may choose to store API keys in our secure vault. These keys:
- Are encrypted using AES-256-GCM before storage
- Are only used to fulfill your requests to third-party APIs
- Remain your property at all times

### 1.3 Application Content

When you create apps on our platform, we store:
- HTML, CSS, and JavaScript code you upload
- App configuration and settings
- Logs of app deployment and changes

### 1.4 Usage Data

We automatically collect:
- **Request logs** — API proxy requests (timestamp, endpoint, success/failure)
- **Access logs** — Pages visited, features used
- **Device information** — Browser type, OS, IP address
- **Performance metrics** — Response times, error rates

### 1.5 Communications

If you contact us, we retain:
- Email content
- Support ticket history
- Feedback and feature requests

## 3. API Key Security

### 3.1 Encryption

All API keys are encrypted using:
- **AES-256-GCM** encryption for data at rest
- **TLS 1.2+** for data in transit
- Secure key management with access controls

### 3.2 Access Control

Your API keys are only accessed when:
- You make an authenticated request through our proxy
- You view or manage keys through your secure dashboard
- Authorized support staff assist you (logged and audited)

### 3.3 Our Commitments

We will never:
- Share, sell, or rent your API keys to third parties
- Use your API keys for purposes other than your requests
- Expose your keys in logs, errors, or responses

### 3.4 Your Responsibilities

You should:
- Regularly review and rotate your API keys
- Remove keys you no longer need
- Use the principle of least privilege when creating API keys

## 4. Data Storage and Security Measures

### 4.1 Where We Store Data

Your data is stored in:
- **United States** in secure data centers
- Encrypted databases with access controls
- Geographically distributed backups

### 4.2 Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security including:
- **Encryption:** All sensitive data encrypted at rest and in transit
- **Access controls:** Role-based access, multi-factor authentication for staff
- **Monitoring:** 24/7 security monitoring and alerting
- **Auditing:** Regular security assessments and penetration testing
- **Backups:** Encrypted backups with separate key management

### 4.3 Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Account data | While account exists + 30 days |
| API keys | While account exists (deleted immediately on account closure) |
| App code | While account exists + 7 days |
| Request logs | 90 days (anonymized after 30 days) |
| Error logs | 30 days |
| Marketing data | Until opt-out + 30 days |

## 6. Your Rights

### 6.1 Access Your Data

You can access your personal data at any time through:
- Your dashboard (apps, settings, API keys)
- Email request to privacy@onhyper.io

### 6.2 Correct Your Data

You can update your information through:
- Account settings in your dashboard
- Email request to privacy@onhyper.io

### 6.3 Delete Your Data

You can request deletion by:
- Closing your account through your dashboard
- Emailing privacy@onhyper.io with "Delete My Account"

Upon deletion:
- Your API keys are deleted immediately
- Your apps are unpublished and deleted within 7 days
- Your account data is deleted within 30 days
- Anonymized usage data may be retained for analytics

### 6.4 Export Your Data

You can export your data by:
- Downloading your app code from the dashboard
- Requesting a full export at privacy@onhyper.io

We will provide your data in a machine-readable format within 30 days.

### 6.5 Opt-Out of Marketing

You can opt out of marketing communications by:
- Clicking "unsubscribe" in any marketing email
- Updating your preferences in your account settings
- Contacting privacy@onhyper.io

### 6.6 Regional Rights

**GDPR (EU/EEA):** You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

**CCPA (California):** You have the right to:
- Know what personal information is collected
- Know whether your personal information is sold or disclosed
- Say no to the sale of personal information
- Access your personal information
- Equal service and price, even if you exercise your privacy rights

We do not sell personal information as that term is defined under CCPA.

## 12. Definitions

- **Personal Data:** Information that identifies you as an individual (email address, usage data linked to your account)
- **API Key:** Authentication credentials for third-party services that you store with us
- **Encryption:** Mathematical process of converting data into an unreadable format that can only be read with a secret key
- **Anonymous Data:** Data that cannot reasonably be linked to an individual user
