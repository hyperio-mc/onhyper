## Phase 2: Content Strategy

### Content Pillars (choose 2-3)
1. **AI/Agent News** — share interesting developments, thread on big announcements
2. **Builder Tips** — practical advice on building AI apps, APIs, integrations
3. **Behind the Scenes** — building OnHyper, tech decisions, lessons learned
4. **Industry Takes** — opinions on where AI is going

### Posting Cadence
- **Minimum**: 3-5x/week
- **Ideal**: 1-2x/day (morning + afternoon)
- **Types**: 60% value, 30% engagement, 10% promotional

### Content Formats
- **Single tweets**: Quick tips, observations
- **Threads**: Deep dives, tutorials, product launches
- **Quote tweets**: Agree/disagree with industry news
- **Reply**: Engage with others in the space

---

## Phase 3: Engagement Strategy

### Who to Engage With
- AI/agent builders and founders
- Developers building on OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.
- Tech Twitter influencers in AI space
- Potential users (startups, indie hackers)

### Tactics
1. **Reply to posts** with value-add comments (not just "great post!")
2. **Quote tweet** with your take + add value
3. **Retweet** with brief commentary
4. **Engage in threads** — show expertise

### Hashtags to Watch
- #AI, #ArtificialIntelligence
- #Agents, #AIAgents
- #LLM, #GPT, #Claude
- #BuildInPublic
- #SaaS

---

## Phase 4: Content Calendar Ideas

### Week 1: Introduction
- Tweet 1: Introduce yourself + what you're building
- Tweet 2: Share your "why" (problem you solved)
- Tweet 3: Thread on common API challenges for AI apps

### Week 2: Value Content
- Tweet 1: Tip thread (e.g., "5 API patterns for AI apps")
- Tweet 2: Opinion on agent infra
- Tweet 3: Share a lesson learned

### Week 3: Product
- Tweet 1: Sneak peek / behind the scenes
- Tweet 2: Feature announcement
- Tweet 3: User testimonial or use case

### Week 4: Engagement
- Reply day (respond to industry news)
- Q&A thread
- Share useful resources

---

## Phase 5: LinkedIn (After Twitter Credibility)

### When to Start
- After 30-60 days of Twitter presence
- When you have 100+ followers
- When you have shareable content (blog posts, launches)

### LinkedIn Differences
- More professional tone
- Longer-form content
- Focus on business/enterprise
- Personal stories work well
